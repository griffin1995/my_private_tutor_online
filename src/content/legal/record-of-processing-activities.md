## Processing Activity 1: Student Educational Services

### 1.1 Processing Details
- **Activity Name:** Student Educational Services Management
- **Purpose:** Providing personalized tutoring services and educational support
- **Legal Basis:**
  - Contractual necessity (Article 6(1)(b))
  - Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) for educational progress tracking
  - Consent (Article 6(1)(a)) for under-16s with parental consent

### 1.2 Data Categories
**Personal Data:**
- Student name and contact details
- Date of birth and age
- Educational background and academic level
- Learning objectives and requirements
- Academic progress and assessment records

**Special Category Data:**
- Special educational needs information (where applicable)
- Learning disabilities or support requirements (with explicit consent)

### 1.3 Data Subjects
- Students (all ages)
- Parents/guardians (for under-16s)

### 1.4 Data Sources
- Direct collection from students/parents
- Educational institutions (with consent)
- Previous tutoring records (with consent)

### 1.5 Data Recipients
- Assigned tutors (on need-to-know basis)
- Administrative staff
- Parents/guardians (progress updates)

### 1.6 Data Transfers
- **UK-based:** All primary data storage within UK
- **International:** None, unless explicitly consented for international tutoring services

### 1.7 Retention Period
- **Academic records:** 7 years after last lesson
- **Special educational needs data:** As long as educationally relevant, maximum 10 years

### 1.8 Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for all communications
- Secure cloud storage with UK-based providers
- Access controls and user authentication
- Regular security audits
