❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions from parents about 11+ preparation

When should we start 11+ preparation?

We recommend starting in Year 5 for a structured, stress-free approach that builds confidence over time. However, our intensive programmes are highly effective for Year 6 students, with many achieving excellent results even when starting just 6 months before the exam.

Which grammar schools do you prepare for?

We cover all major grammar schools including Tiffin (Boys & Girls), St. Albans, Henrietta Barnett, Latymer, Dame Alice Owen's, and many more across London and the Home Counties. Our tutors are familiar with the specific requirements of each school and exam board (CEM, GL Assessment, CSSE).

What makes your approach different from other 11+ tutors?

Our tutors specialize exclusively in 11+ preparation and understand the specific requirements of each exam board and school. We provide personalized learning plans, regular mock exams, detailed progress tracking, and most importantly, we focus on building your child's confidence alongside their academic skills.

How do you help with exam anxiety?

We incorporate confidence-building techniques throughout our programme, including regular mock exams in realistic conditions, breathing and relaxation techniques, positive visualization, and time management strategies. Many of our students report feeling excited rather than nervous about their exam day.
